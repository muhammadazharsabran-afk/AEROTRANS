About AeroTrans Logistics Sdn. Bhd.

Your trusted partner in logistics and transportation solutions

Our Company Description

AeroTrans Logistics Sdn. Bhd. uses its logo design to express three core values which are speed and reliability and professional conduct. The truck represents the company's transportation and delivery services, while the airplane shows the company's ability to deliver fast and extensive logistical operations. The curved lines show movement and smooth delivery processes.

The blue color represents trust and professionalism and the grey color indicates stability and reliability. The logo displays a bold company name which helps people to identify the logo with ease. The logo demonstrates AeroTrans Logistics' dedication to delivering effective and trustworthy logistics solutions.

Strategic Location

The location of AeroTrans Logistics Sdn. Bhd. in Shah Alam Selangor establishes the company as one of Malaysia's key industrial and commercial centers. The city of Shah Alam provides direct connections to major roadways which include the Federal Highway and North-South Expressway and New Klang Valley Expressway (NKVE) system.

Location Advantages

  • Direct access to manufacturing facilities and storage locations
  • Better control over transportation activities
  • Reduced delivery times and simplified procedures
  • Infrastructure supports heavy vehicle operations
  • Enhanced service efficiency and expanded coverage area
